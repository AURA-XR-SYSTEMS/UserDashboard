# AURA ENGINE — PRIVACY POLICY

**Last Updated:** July 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Aura XR Systems LLC** ("**AURA**," "**we**," "**us**") collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information in connection with the AURA Engine platform and **https://auraengine.com** (the "**Service**"). It applies to visitors, account holders, and Authorized Users globally, and includes specific disclosures for **California (CCPA/CPRA)** and the **EU/UK (GDPR)**.

This Policy also serves as our **"Notice at Collection"** under the CCPA/CPRA.

> **Roles.** When we handle personal information of our own visitors and customers, we act as a **business/controller**. When we process **Customer Data** that a business customer uploads to the Service on behalf of that customer, we generally act as a **service provider/processor**, and that customer's privacy policy and our **Data Processing Addendum (DPA)** govern that data.

## 2. Sensitive Personal Information (CPRA)

We may process **precise geolocation** and, depending on Customer Data you upload, other information that could be "sensitive." We use Sensitive Personal Information **only** to provide and secure the Service and for the purposes permitted under Cal. Civ. Code § 1798.121 — **not** to infer characteristics about you. We do **not** sell or share Sensitive Personal Information, and we honor the right to limit its use (Section 8).

## 3. How We Use Personal Information (Purposes)

- Provide, operate, maintain, and secure the Service and your account;
- Process payments and manage subscriptions and automatic renewals;
- Generate Output (digital twins, analyses, visualizations) from Customer Data;
- Provide support and respond to communications;
- Monitor performance, prevent fraud and abuse, and ensure security;
- Improve and develop the Service and our AI models **using only aggregated, anonymized, or de-identified data — we do not use your raw or identifiable Customer Data to train our AI models** (see Section 4);
- Send service and transactional messages, renewal reminders, and (with consent where required) marketing;
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

**Legal bases (GDPR/UK GDPR):** performance of a contract; our legitimate interests (securing and improving the Service); your consent (where required, e.g., certain cookies/marketing); and compliance with legal obligations.

## 4. AI and Model Improvement

We use AI/ML to provide features such as conversational spatial queries and document spatialization. We may use **aggregated, anonymized, or de-identified** data derived from use of the Service to evaluate and improve our models and platform. We do not use identifiable Customer Data to train our models, and we do not attempt to re-identify de-identified data. Some AI features may rely on third-party model providers (Section 6), who process inputs under their own terms and, where applicable, our agreements with them.

## 5. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies for authentication, preferences, security, and analytics. We use analytics services to understand usage. You can control cookies through your browser and, where offered, our cookie banner. We honor **Global Privacy Control (GPC)** signals as a valid opt-out of "sale"/"sharing" where applicable.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described, then delete or de-identify it. General guidelines: account and billing records for the life of the account plus **7 years** for tax/legal purposes; **automatic-renewal consent records for at least three years** (as required by California law); Customer Data per your Terms (typically up to **30 days** after termination, then deletion); logs for **12 months**. Specific periods vary by category and legal requirement.

## 8. Your California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have the right to:

- **Delete** personal information we hold, subject to exceptions;
- **Correct** inaccurate personal information;
- **Opt out of sale/sharing** (we do not sell or share, but you may still register a preference / use GPC);
- **Limit use of Sensitive Personal Information** to permitted purposes;
- **Non-discrimination** for exercising your rights.

**How to exercise:** email **privacy@auraengine.com**. **Categories disclosed for a business purpose in the last 12 months:** Identifiers, Commercial information, Internet/network activity, Geolocation, and any personal information contained in Customer Data — each disclosed to the service providers listed in Section 6.
